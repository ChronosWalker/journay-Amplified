Some Amplified Journeys and My Services

  • African Inspired Fashion

    Christine Eruokwu is a business professional with years of experience in the education and non-profit sectors. Her passion to empower women and young girls to maximize their potential and lead with purpose drew her to begin a fashion business. She sends the proceeds to Africa where the funds are used towards school uniforms for girls. Her story inspires us to use what we have to bless the less advantaged around us.

  • African Inspired Food

    Bolaji Akintola is an Accountant by profession, but also an entrepreneur who ventured into food retail after relocating to Canada and discovered the gap in supply of African foods. Her research path despite being in early stage of settlement gave her a boost to succeed as a newcomer in New Brunswick.

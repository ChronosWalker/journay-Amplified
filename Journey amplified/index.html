The Iconic damilola

In the heart of New Brunswick, Damilola, a Nigerian-born Canadian who found her forever home here, noticed a space waiting to be filled. Newcomers, brimming with potential, were arriving but perhaps missing a piece of the puzzle – the stories of those who had walked a similar path before them. Damilola decided to bridge this gap with the magic of storytelling. She weaves captivating narratives, using interviews to capture the inspiring journeys of new Canadians, not just in New Brunswick, but across the vast Canadian landscape. Through these stories, she doesn't just inform, she amplifies the voices of those who dared to chase a dream in a new land, inspiring newcomers and fostering a sense of belonging for all.
